Yang Liu

I received the B.E. degree in Software Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University, Shaanxi, China in 2016 and the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science advised by Prof. Zhenjiang Li from City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, China in 2020. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher working with Dr. Zhenjiang Li in the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong.

I am focusing on developing intelligent mobile and wearable sensing technologies for Internet of Things (IoT) systems with signal processing and Artificial Intelligence (AI) techniques to enable applications of Human Computer Interaction (HCI) and smart health. I am also interested in cyber security and privacy, where I mainly concerns privacy leakage issues in mobile and wearable sensing, and adversarial attacks in Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS).
